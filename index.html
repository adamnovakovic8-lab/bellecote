Why come

One little village, a whole lot to do

From sunrise paddles to evening kitchen parties, every day mixes the outdoors with cozy indoor happenings — rain or shine.

Live music

Fiddlers, songwriters and Saturday night kitchen parties on the waterfront stage.

Outdoor adventures

Beach games, guided coastal hikes, kayak tours and a sandcastle showdown.

Food & market

Lobster rolls, local makers and a farmers' market under the marquee.

Family fun

Craft tents, face painting and a kids' parade — free activities all week.

Since1987 · 38 summers
Our story

A seaside tradition, made by neighbours

What started as a one-afternoon community picnic has grown into the highlight of the Belle Côte summer. It's still run entirely by volunteers, and it's still about the same thing: bringing people together along this stretch of the Ceilidh Trail.

Whether you grew up here or you're discovering Cape Breton for the first time, there's a seat at the table for you.
